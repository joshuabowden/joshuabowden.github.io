URBAN GROW

Web Design

Overview

A website that can be used for tutorials on growing miniature plants such as Cacti, Bonsai and Pumpkins. The style of design is similar to that of a blog. My goal with this project was to experiment in web design and continue to develop my own style. The outcome is a responsive website design and is ongoing to become a fully functional live project.

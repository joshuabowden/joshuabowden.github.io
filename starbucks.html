8 June, 2015

Above is a mockup featuring the project designs.

Overview

As a small interface project I set my self the challenge of re-designing some of the Starbucks website. The idea of this project was to explore a flat style of design and experiment with my own creative design styles. This included things such as altering the content layout and using a wider variation of colour throughout the pages.

Fully Responsive (e.g. Smartphones, Tablets)

Apple Music

19 November, 2015

Final Design: Displayed on a mockup of an iPhone

OVERVIEW

A re-design of Apple's Music Application. The purpose of this project was to explore a concept idea that removes a lot of features from an app and replaces them with a creative User Interface. Touch gestures could be used in a different way than usual with the user simply pressing and holding down on the bottom part of the screen to reveal options such as Shuffle and Loop. Swipe gestures could be used for switching songs.

Features

Compatible with Smartphones

Touch and Swipe Gestures

OUTCOME

Final Designs

The design outcome are two different screen designs showing the user interface of the application. The colour red has been used within the design to match the colours and theme of both the song being played and artwork featured. The designs feature a music player re-design showing a playback screen and song list.

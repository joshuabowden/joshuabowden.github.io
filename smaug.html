Smaug - 'Photo Retouch'

24 February, 2015

Final Photo: Edited/Re-Touched in Photoshop

OVERVIEW

A photo-manipulation project; retouch of an original screenshot taken from the film 'The Hobbit: Desolation of Smaug'. The idea os this project design has been to re-create a scene within the film that features a dragon breathing fire. My goals were also to improve the shot and create it as a good standard for a digital wallpaper. This adding a variety of colour corrections and manipulating the original photo by adding extras and re-creating the fire effect. This took a couple of hours through the use of clone stamps and various layer styles, brushes etc.

River Island

30 September, 2016

Final Design: Displayed on a mockup of an iMac

OVERVIEW

A splash screen/home page re-design of the River Island website. Some of the ideas for this project were to create a simple design and experiment with the spacing between content.

Features

Compatible with Desktops

User-Friendly Navigation

Minimalistic Interface

OUTCOME

The outcome of this project is an animated design concept that shows some of the funtionality which could be used within this design. This has been produced as a .gif through After Effects. The project could also be extended further in the future with a re-design of the other parts of the River Island website.
